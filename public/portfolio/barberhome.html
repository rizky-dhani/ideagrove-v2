Our story

Built by barbers,
not investors

BLK & GOLD started in 2019 when two friends pooled their savings and a stubborn refusal to compromise. Marcus had 12 years behind the chair. Dev had the eye for space and atmosphere.

We built the shop we wanted to work in. Good light, honest products, music that doesn't grate. No franchise playbook, no upsell. Just the craft, done well, every time.
